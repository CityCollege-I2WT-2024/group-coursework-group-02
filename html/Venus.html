Welcome to Venus

• Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often referred to as Earth's "sister planet" due to its similar size and composition. Despite this, it has a hostile environment with extreme temperatures and clouds of sulfuric acid.
• Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere made primarily of carbon dioxide (96.5%) with clouds of sulfuric acid.
• The Atmospheric Pressure is about 92 times that of Earth, equivalent to being 900 meters underwater on Earth.
•Venus is one of two planets (along with Mercury) that has no moons or ring systems.
•Unlike Earth, Venus lacks a strong magnetic field. Scientists believe this is due to its slow rotation and the absence of a solid inner core generating a dynamo effect.
•The upper atmosphere features winds that can reach speeds of up to 360 km/h.

Intersting facts

Jupiter Has Rings Too!

While Saturn is famous for its rings, Jupiter also has a faint ring system composed mainly of dust. These rings are difficult to see because they are so thin and dim.

Home to 95 Moons

As of 2025, Jupiter has 95 confirmed moons, including four massive ones called the Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
Ganymede is the largest moon in the solar system and even larger than Mercury.
Europa is thought to have a subsurface ocean that might harbor extraterrestrial life.

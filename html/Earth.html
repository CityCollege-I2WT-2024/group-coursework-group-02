Earth: The Living World

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to support life.

Earth has a unique atmosphere composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen, which helps sustain life and regulate temperature.

Earth's surface is 71% water, with the rest being land, including mountains, plains, and deserts. It has a dynamic climate system and is home to millions of species, making it incredibly diverse.

About Me

The Project

Regarding the project, for me it was my first big scale project compared to the ones I was used to doing in high-school. It was an amazing experience which lead to new friendships being formed while also enjoying the whole process of bringing to life what was initially just drawn on a blank whiteboard as you can see in the image to the right. I suppose we came very close.

We worked a lot as a team even though we worked on a lot of individual parts separately.
